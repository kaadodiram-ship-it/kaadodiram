About Ramakrishnan V / Kaadodi Ram

Economist, Tamil Writer and Poet

Completed Mechanical Engineering and Post Graduation in Economics. Having strong interest for public life, prepared for UPSC Civil Services Examination and failed. Despite failure, trying to explore my strong interest in drama & theatre writing and acting, writing poems, short stories, and cinema screen writing.

In addition to that, exploring research space in Economics and writing articles about financial and social economics.

My work spans across multiple domains including economic analysis, socio-political commentary, and creative writing in both Tamil and English.

English Poetry

Sample poems in English

Here are some of my English poems that explore themes of conflict, peace, and human consciousness.

1.

The war began —

No,

It was only a test of weapons.

Let them go to the marketplace;

Every weapon strikes there.

The killer was Russian,

The dead was Russian.

2.

A ceasefire was signed —

No.

Peace was successfully sold.

3.

My liberation lies

In leaving behind

The burden of thought I carried.

4.

Reading — a waterfall

Cascading down the mountain peaks of the brain;

From there is born a river of thought,

And from there, mankind.

Social economics examines the relationship between economic activity and social life. My research in this area focuses on how economic policies affect social welfare and community development.

Areas of interest include:

  • Income inequality
